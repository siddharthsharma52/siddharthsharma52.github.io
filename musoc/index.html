Crescendo: The Music Society of NSIT



I was the President of the Music Society of NSIT during my junior year in college. As president, I was responsible for bringing significant improvement in terms of activity and presence to a virtually defunct society. To establish a stable administration, I appointed a team of 6 members out of a total of around 50 members in the society. During that year, we had a successful stint at Rendezvous 2013, Annual Cultural Fest of IIT - Delhi, with 2nd position in Solo Western Vocals, 3rd position in Solo Instrumentals and participation in Group Western Vocals We also orgnized Crescendo Eve in September, 2012, with 15 musical performances of varied genres by society members. Crescendo Eve is now an annual event at NSIT
